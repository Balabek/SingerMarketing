About Singer Marketing

Singer marketing is a full service performance marketing agency with core capabilities across email, search, and social.

Specializing in B2C marketing for the automotive and ecommerce verticals, Singer Marketing leverages the power of our people-based marketing platform in conjunction with the world’s largest marketing platforms. By activating your true buying audience, we prioritizes revenue and customer growth in every campaign and initiative for our clients.

In a world of endless options, Singer Marketing makes it easy to find your ideal customer.

Automotive Services

We help auto dealers reach their audiences everywhere - email, social, desktop, mobile, search, connected TV - wherever they are, is where we put you.
